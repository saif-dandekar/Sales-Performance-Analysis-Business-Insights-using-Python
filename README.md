📊 Sales Data Analysis Project
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a sales dataset to identify key business insights, sales trends, and performance patterns.
The objective is to understand which regions, products, and categories drive the most revenue and profit and provide actionable recommendations for business growth.

🎯 Business Objective

The main goals of this analysis are:

Identify top-performing regions

Analyze best-selling product categories

Determine high-demand products

Evaluate overall profitability

Provide data-driven business recommendations

📈 Key Insights

The West region generated the highest total sales.

Technology is the best-performing product category.

The top-selling product is Canon imageCLASS Printer.

The average profit per order is $28.65, suggesting moderate profitability.

💡 Business Recommendations

Increase marketing efforts in the West region to maximize revenue.
